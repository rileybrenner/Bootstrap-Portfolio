About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Riley Brenner. I am a 26 year old software developer who is currently located in Berkeley, California. I am originally from a small beach town in New York outside of Queens called Long Beach. I currently work at Adobe as an Application Support Engineer.My undegrad studies were at the University of North Carolina at Wilmington. Wilmington is a port city on the southern coast of North Carolina. I have a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business with a concentration in Marketing & Advertising. I currently work at Adobe in the Bay Area of California. Much of my free time is spent exploring outside. My passion in life is surfing. I started at an early age in elementary school in the temperate waters of Northeastern United States. Some of my other hobbies include hiking, snowboarding, paddle boarding, diving and fishing. My passions have taken me across the globe traveling to over a dozen different countries over the past few years. I sometimes find myself searching in some of the most desolate corners of the planet in search of the new frontier for waves and exploration.
